About The Sukoon Lab

The Sukoon Lab is an independent music project dedicated to keeping timeless Urdu poetry alive through modern sound. Every release blends classic literature with cinematic production, indie music and soulful reinterpretations inspired by legends like Mirza Ghalib, Parveen Shakir, Bashir Badr, Jaun Elia and many more.
