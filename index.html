About The Sukoon Lab

The Sukoon Lab is dedicated to bringing timeless Urdu poetry to a new generation through modern music. Inspired by the works of Mirza Ghalib, Bashir Badr, Jaun Elia, Parveen Shakir and many other legendary poets, every release blends classic literature with contemporary soundscapes. Whether it is a ghazal, a nostalgic retro reinterpretation or an original composition, every song is created with one purpose—to keep beautiful poetry alive.
